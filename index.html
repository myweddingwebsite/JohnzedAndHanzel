Our Love Story

Eight years, three proposals, and one forever answer.

❤

Where It All Began

Our story started in TESDA during a Red Cross bloodletting activity. My mom and Johnzed were classmates in Cookery class. She brought me there to participate in the blood donation activity and introduced me to everyone in her class — including Johnzed. That simple introduction became the beginning of our love story.

💍

Finally Official

Johnzed promised my mom that he would only ask me to be his girlfriend after I graduated from college. True to his word, after two years of patiently courting me, he asked me on the day of my graduation if I would finally become his girlfriend. I happily said yes.

💖

The Proposal

Eight years into our relationship, we experienced countless ups and downs. Johnzed proposed not once, not twice, but three times. He respected every "not yet" because he knew I wanted the moment to be truly meaningful. In 2025, inside the home we built together, surrounded by the people who mattered most, and with a tattoo serving as our engagement ring, he asked one final time. It was simple. It was heartfelt. It was everything I had dreamed of. And I finally said yes.

💒

Forever Begins

As we begin this new chapter together, our wish remains simple — to celebrate our love surrounded by the people who have always cared for us. There may be unexpected moments along the journey, but what excites us the most is sharing this unforgettable day with all of you.
